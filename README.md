## Tax Year

All rates in the study guide reflect **2026/27 SA tax year** (SARS Budget Feb 2026):
- CGT annual exclusion alive: R50,000
- CGT exclusion on death: R440,000
- Primary residence exclusion: R3,000,000
- Small business exclusion: R2,700,000
- Estate duty abatement: R3,500,000 (R7M portable)
- Estate duty rate: 20% (up to R30M) / 25% (above)

Past exam papers preserve the rates of their respective years for authenticity.
